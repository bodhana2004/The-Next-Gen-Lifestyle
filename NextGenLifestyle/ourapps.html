HOME

Next Gen Home will aid in providing long-term plans and short-term solutions for everything and anything that could happen in an individual’s life, including planning out where to buy home necessities, a ‘green’ cleaning routine, where and how to have a sustainable vacation, limiting water usage, redesigning your living space, etc.

NEXT GEN

BUSINESS

Next Gen Business will be all about planning out how company offices are built, how electricity and water usage can be reduced and the company can transition to using renewable resources, cleaner deliveries and product production, business trips, etc.

NEXT GEN

KIDS

Next Gen Kids will be designed to develop the creative thinking as well as imagination of our youngest generation. From interactive projects and games to daily lessons about environmental awareness and sustainability, kids will be taught from the beginning that nature always comes first.

NEXT GEN

TEENS

Next Gen Teens will focus on making the daily routines of students in high school and college sustainable. To keep track of his/her time online, visual representations, such as the amount of pollutants released from their actions, as well as challenges to limit their time using the Internet will be available.
